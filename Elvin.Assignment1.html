Patented Technology

Tested on Brain and Behavior

Brain.Fm holds patents on key processes for creating fuctional music, including technology to elicit strong neutral phase locking- allowing populations of neurons to engage in various kinds of coordinated activity- and technology to remove and distract sound.

This makes our music unique, purpose-built to steer you into a desired mental state. In other words we found new ways to create music that helps you do what you need.

Explore More of Our Science

Scientists at Brain.Fm work with collaborators at academic institution to run experiments.

We look at the effects of our technology on the brain, using fMRI and EEG, and also run large-scale behavoiral test, measuring performance on simple games.

Our experiments allways include a controlled condition ('placebo') of the same underlining music without Brain.Fm technology applied, so we can make sure our tech is what makes the difference.
